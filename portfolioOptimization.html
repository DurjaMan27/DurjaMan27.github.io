Investment Portfolio Optimization

Check out our code here!

August 2020 Varun Rao

After nearly 4 months of hard data science work during quarantine, the Glen Pi Geeks were finally ready to start working on a new challenge: Python for Finance. Assisted by the book "Modern Portfolio Theory and Investment Analysis" published by Wiley, we were able to learn the basics of investment portfolios and what they entail. We brushed up on the basics of trading in portfolios and different statistics that we might have to monitor, like correlation, covariance, and volatility. Using our newfound knowledge, we progressed to analyzing a group of stocks of our choosing, determining the maximum amount of returns for the group and the specific allocation of funds designed to maximize returns.
